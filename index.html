Here's our story.

Originally the dream of Italian immigrants, Copolla's Pizzeria was founded by our grandparents who came to Bemus Point, New York from their small town in Collepietro, Italy. The restaurant has since been passed down three generations and still utilizes the original family recipes, particularly the homemade dough and tomato sauce.
